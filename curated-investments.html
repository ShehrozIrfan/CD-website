Curated Investments

Extraordinary access to proprietary investment portfolios constructed using individual security selection.

commons pillars

Commons Capital Advisors Investment Management focuses on individual equities and bonds, preferred stocks, convertible securities, REITs and hard assets. We are in constant pursuit of
achieving above-average growth whilst preserving principal. Our priority is managing excess risk by crafting well diversified portfolios which contain assets that are not highly correlated in price movements with each other.

Commons Capital Advisors Investment Management services provides bespoke portfolios that follow a rigorous and disciplined approach while remaining efficient and cost-effective.

growth EQUITY portfolio

Strong sustainable sales and earnings growth, dominant market share within their industries, strong balance sheets and defendable business models.

growth & income equity portfolio

Total return approach from liquid, well-known industry leaders that generally have market capitalizations in excess of $20 billion.

income equity portfolio

Designed for moderate investors who want to obtain an ongoing secure income stream from what we view as dependable sources, with some emphasis on protection of principal.

multi-asset portfolio

Balancing a combination of many different types of asset classes, geographies and styles to create a more nimble and diversified portfolio to achieve our clients respective investment objectives.

fixed income portfolio

The primary objective is current income and reasonable stability of principal to support overall long-term portfolio performance, reduce volatility and maintain desired lifestyle.
